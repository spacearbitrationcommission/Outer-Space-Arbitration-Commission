太空仲裁委员会仲裁条款

Outer Space Arbitration Commission Arbitration Clause

因本合同引起的或与本合同有关的争议，应当提交太空仲裁委员会，按申请仲裁时该会现行有效的仲裁规则进行仲裁。仲裁裁决是终局的，对各方均有约束力。Any dispute arising from or related to this contract should be submitted to the Outer Space Arbitration Commission, and arbitrated in accordance with the arbitration rules in effect at the time of the application for arbitration. The arbitration decision is final and binding on all parties.
